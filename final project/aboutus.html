About Us

In the 1920s and 1930s, Carlsbad become a major producer of vegetables, fruits and flowers which were shipped all over the country from this depot. Bountiful Foods is a long time food and nutrition store in Carlsbad, Carlifonia and was and was started by the current owner and operator , Sally Borenstein. Bountiful Foods does not sell genetically modified foods.
